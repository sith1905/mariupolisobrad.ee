Mariupol's Friends

A charity volunteer organisation, helping Ukrainian war refugees who came to Estonia.

Donate
We are a charity organisation, which was created by a group of volunteers when the problem of transit war refugees in Estonia became obvious.

Many families are disoriented, not knowing what to do and where to go. Some of them don't plan to stay in Estonia, they want to reunite with their relatives in Europe or other countries around the world to build a new life there.

We help in any possible way we can. 

If you go to Estonia (whether you go transit or not) and you need help – please fill out our form in advance and we will try to contact you as soon as possible.



If you are going to Estonia or passing through, here are some examples of what we can help with:
  • Consult about available options for refugees in Estonia
  • Plan the optimal route and transport, suggest available options in another countries
  • Answer questions about transit requirements
  • Help with everyday issues on the spot, to retrieve something
  • Help with accommodation or tickets 
  • Tell where and when to find the volunteers
  • Discuss challenging issues and find a solution

    Transit refugees need urgent help. Governmental support programs are designated only for those who have decided to stay in Estonia, thus a large number of families turn to us. The amount of people is not decreasing, and families are constantly in need of attention and help from volunteers, both online and offline. We are ready to teach, help, answer questions and share all the information and tools available to our organisation for volunteers, who would join us to help families.
